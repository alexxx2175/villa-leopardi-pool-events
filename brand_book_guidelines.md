## Villa Leopardi Design System Guidelines

### 1. Color Palette

The brand utilizes a refined and muted color palette, emphasizing elegance and minimalism.

*   **Primary Background**
    *   **Hex:** `#bdb1a1`
    *   **RGB:** (189, 177, 161)
    *   **CMYK:** (27, 26, 34, 7)
    *   **Role:** Main background for branding materials, darker sections, and elements requiring a sophisticated, earthy tone.

*   **Secondary Background / Light Text**
    *   **Hex:** `#dbd8cf`
    *   **RGB:** (219, 216, 207)
    *   **CMYK:** (16, 13, 19, 0)
    *   **Role:** Lighter background elements, subtle accents, and text color when placed on the primary background (`#bdb1a1`).

*   **Dark Text / Primary Elements**
    *   **Hex:** `#464543`
    *   **RGB:** (70, 69, 67)
    *   **CMYK:** (63, 54, 54, 54)
    *   **Role:** Primary text color on light backgrounds (`#f5f5f5` or `#dbd8cf`), main logo color on light backgrounds, dark elements, and branding accents.

*   **Pure White / Lightest Background**
    *   **Hex:** `#f5f5f5`
    *   **RGB:** (245, 245, 245)
    *   **CMYK:** (5, 4, 4, 0)
    *   **Role:** Clean backgrounds, negative space, and elements requiring a stark white appearance.
